How to Use

To use mycGPA calculator, follow the steps below:
  • Fill in the fields with your course code, course unit and grade
  • Click the 'Add Course' button to add more courses
  • Click the 'Add Semester' button to add new fields for other semesters
  • After you fill the fields for the semesters, the cGPA is displayed automatically at the bottom right.
  • You have successfully calculated your cGPA.
